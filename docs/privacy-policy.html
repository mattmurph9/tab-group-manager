Privacy Policy

Tab Group Manager Chrome Extension

TL;DR: Tab Group Manager does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data remains on your device.

Data Collection

Tab Group Manager does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information.

The extension operates entirely locally on your device and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Storage

Tab Group Manager uses Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.sync) to save your tab group rules and configuration. This data includes:

  • Tab group rules (URL patterns, group names, colors)
  • Rule enable/disable status
  • No personal information or browsing history

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is synced across your Chrome browsers (if you have Chrome sync enabled in your browser settings)
  • Is never sent to external servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or analytics

Permissions

Tab Group Manager requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: Required to access tab information and organize tabs into groups
  • tabGroups: Required to create and manage tab groups
  • storage: Required to save your rules configuration
  • <all_urls>: Required to check URLs of tabs to match against your rules

The extension only uses these permissions for their stated purpose: automatically organizing your tabs into groups based on your configured rules. No URL data is collected, logged, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Tab Group Manager does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, tracking scripts, or external APIs. The extension is completely self-contained and operates entirely within your browser.

Updates to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
